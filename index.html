Our mehtodology, The Chasm, allows us to connect with entrepreneurs and assist them in the process of creating their own companies. Through hackathons, contests, market research and design thinking sessions we look for new ideas and entrepreneurial talents. From there on, and with our mentoring, we support them in order to accelerate their development in the market. We work closely in building the business strategy and put together multidisciplinary teams, leveraging our technological know-how with our strategic partner ecosystem.
