Why Parabolt?

Nowadays, there are plenty of incubating, accelerating and company building options for startups. However, Parabolt offers something unique: the support of gA, a company with 25 years of experience in business, industries and technologies. gA knows what it's like to be an entrepreneur: it was born as a start up. Thanks to this support, Parabolt knows what to do in order to transform ideas into meaningful solutions that impact people's everyday life.

At Parabolt, you can:

  • Present your idea so we can help you make it real
  • Show us your product to be accelerated in the market
  • Develop your start up so it can grow to be the company of your dreams
